Admissions & Enrollment

Starlight Primary welcomes families who are looking for a vibrant and caring school community. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward, helping us understand how we can best support your child's learning journey.

Applications for the upcoming school year open on October 1st and close on December 15th. We invite prospective parents and children to visit our school during our open house events to experience our unique atmosphere firsthand.

Required documents typically include your child's birth certificate, immunization records, and any previous school reports. Please contact our admissions office for a detailed checklist and to schedule a tour.

About Starlight Primary

Established in 2077, Starlight Primary was founded with a vision to provide a joyful and enriching educational experience for young children. We believe that learning should be an adventure, where every child feels valued, supported, and excited to discover new things.

Our modern campus features bright classrooms, a vibrant playground, a dedicated arts studio, and interactive learning zones designed to stimulate young minds. Our passionate educators are experts in early childhood development, committed to nurturing each child's individual talents and fostering a lifelong love of learning.

At Starlight Primary, we partner with families to create a strong foundation for academic success, social-emotional well-being, and creative expression, ensuring every child's journey is filled with wonder and achievement.
