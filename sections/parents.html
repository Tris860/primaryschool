Enrollment Cost & Fees

Starlight Primary is committed to providing exceptional education at an accessible value. Our fees cover tuition, standard learning materials, and access to all core school facilities and activities.

Annual Tuition Fees (2077-2078 Academic Year)

  • Early Years Foundation Stage: $8,500
  • Key Stage 1 (Years 1-2): $9,200
  • Key Stage 2 (Years 3-6): $9,800

Additional Costs (Estimated)

  • Uniform Package: $200 - $350 (one-time purchase)
  • School Trips & Excursions: $50 - $150 per year (varies by year group)
  • After-School Clubs (Optional): $75 - $150 per term (varies by club)

A non-refundable application fee of $50 is required upon submission of your child's application. For detailed payment plans and scholarship opportunities, please contact our admissions office directly.
