Our Academic Journey

At Starlight Primary, we believe in a vibrant and engaging curriculum that sparks curiosity and builds a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Discover how we inspire our young learners every day.

Curriculum Overview

Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

Our Early Years program focuses on learning through play, fostering social skills, early literacy, and numeracy. Children explore seven areas of learning, developing a love for discovery in a nurturing environment.

  • Communication and Language
  • Physical Development
  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development
  • Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • Understanding the World
  • Expressive Arts and Design

Key Stage 1 (Years 1-2)

In Key Stage 1, we build upon the EYFS foundation, introducing more structured learning in core subjects like English, Mathematics, and Science. Our cross-curricular approach ensures learning is interconnected and meaningful.

  • English (Reading, Writing, Phonics)
  • Mathematics (Number, Measurement, Geometry)
  • Science (Plants, Animals, Materials)
  • History, Geography, Art, Music, PE, Computing

Key Stage 2 (Years 3-6)

Key Stage 2 deepens understanding and encourages independent learning. Students engage in challenging projects, critical thinking, and collaborative activities across a broad range of subjects, preparing them for secondary education.

  • Advanced English and Mathematics
  • In-depth Science Investigations
  • World History and Geography
  • Modern Foreign Languages (e.g., French, Spanish)
  • Advanced Computing and Digital Literacy

Our Teaching Methods & Philosophy

At Starlight Primary, our teaching philosophy is centered around creating active, engaged, and enthusiastic learners. We employ a blend of innovative and proven methods to cater to diverse learning styles:

Inquiry-Based Learning

We encourage students to ask questions, investigate, and discover answers independently, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Collaborative & Project-Based Learning

Students work together on exciting projects, developing teamwork, communication, and presentation skills while tackling real-world challenges.

Personalized Learning Pathways

Recognizing that every child is unique, we tailor our teaching to individual needs, providing differentiated instruction and support to ensure every student thrives.

Assessment & Progress

At Starlight Primary, assessment is an ongoing process designed to support and celebrate each child's learning journey. We use a variety of methods to understand your child's progress, focusing on their individual growth and next steps.

Formative Assessment

Teachers continuously observe and interact with students, providing immediate feedback and adjusting teaching to meet individual needs. This includes daily observations, questioning, and short activities.

Summative Assessment & Reporting

Formal assessments are conducted periodically to evaluate overall understanding and progress. Parents receive regular reports and have opportunities for parent-teacher conferences to discuss their child's development.

Our goal is to ensure every child feels confident and supported as they progress through their academic journey at Starlight Primary.
